# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard & Forecasting (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive sales analytics dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze the performance of a retail superstore. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, order quantity, shipping performance, and regional trends.

In addition to historical analysis, the project also includes a **15-day sales forecast** using time-series forecasting to help stakeholders anticipate future sales trends and make better business decisions.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how raw business data can be transformed into **actionable insights through data visualization and business intelligence tools**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance of the superstore
* Identify trends in **profit, sales, and order quantity**
* Understand **regional and category-wise sales distribution**
* Monitor **shipping performance and payment methods**
* Forecast future sales trends for short-term planning

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Microsoft Excel** – Source dataset

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset used in this project contains historical sales records of a retail superstore.

The dataset is included in the **dataset folder** of this repository.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights important business metrics including:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Shipping Time

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

### 2️⃣ Sales Analysis

The dashboard allows users to analyze sales across multiple dimensions:

* Sales by **Region**
* Sales by **State**
* Sales by **Category**
* Sales by **Sub-Category**
* Sales by **Customer Segment**

---

### 3️⃣ Profit Analysis

Profit trends are analyzed through:

* Monthly **Profit Trends**
* Category-wise Profit Distribution
* Region-wise Profit Performance

---

### 4️⃣ Shipping & Payment Insights

The dashboard also provides operational insights such as:

* Sales by **Shipping Mode**
* Sales by **Payment Method**
* Average Shipping Duration

These metrics help understand logistics and customer purchasing behavior.

---

### 5️⃣ Time Series Trend Analysis

The project includes **Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons** for:

* Monthly Sales
* Monthly Profit

This helps identify **seasonal trends and performance changes across years**.

---

### 6️⃣ Sales Forecasting

The second dashboard page includes a **15-day sales forecast** using Power BI’s built-in time-series forecasting.

This feature helps estimate short-term sales trends based on historical data.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

Some key insights derived from the dashboard include:

* The **West region contributes the highest share of total sales**.
* **Office Supplies** is the highest selling product category.
* **Standard Class shipping** is the most commonly used shipping method.
* Sales show a noticeable increase toward the **end of the year**.
* Forecast results indicate **stable short-term sales growth**.
